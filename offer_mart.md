# Tata Capital Offer Mart: Product & Policy Knowledge Base

## 1. Personal Loan Products
### Instant Personal Loan
- **Description**: Fast-track loans for salaried individuals.
- **Interest Rates**: 10.99% - 15.49% per annum.
- **Tenure**: 12 to 72 months.
- **Maximum Amount**: Up to ₹25,00,000.
- **Processing Fee**: 2% of loan amount.

### Home Renovation Loan
- **Description**: Specific loans for home improvements.
- **Interest Rates**: 11.49% - 14.99% per annum.
- **Tenure**: Up to 60 months.

## 2. Underwriting & Eligibility Policies
### General Eligibility
- **Age**: 21 to 58 years.
- **Minimum Monthly Income**: ₹25,000 (Metros), ₹20,000 (Non-metros).
- **Minimum Credit Score**: 700.

### Approval Logic (Ref: Challenge Docs)
1. **Instant Approval**: If requested amount ≤ Pre-approved limit AND Credit Score ≥ 700.
2. **Conditional Approval (Salary Slip Required)**: If requested amount ≤ 2x Pre-approved limit.
   - **Condition**: Expected EMI must be ≤ 50% of monthly salary.
3. **Rejection Cases**:
   - Requested amount > 2x Pre-approved limit.
   - Credit score < 700.

## 3. KYC Requirements
- **Identity Proof**: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, or Passport.
- **Address Proof**: Utility bills, Rental agreement, or Voter ID.
- **Income Proof**: Last 3 months' salary slips and 6 months' bank statements.

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- **Q: Can I prepay my loan?**
  - A: Yes, after 6 months of EMI payments with a 3% foreclosure charge.
- **Q: How long does disbursement take?**
  - A: Usually within 24-48 hours after final approval and sanction letter generation.
